About

I'm an integration and automation engineer with over eight years of experience fixing the things that slow companies down and building systems that help them grow.

Some of the most useful experience I've picked up came from working in startups, sitting close enough to sales, marketing, and operations to understand how the whole machine runs. That experience changed how I approach technical work. I stopped seeing problems as purely technical and started seeing them as business problems first. It's a small shift in perspective that makes a significant difference in whether a solution actually sticks.

My current toolkit covers no-code/low-code platforms, coding, and both AI and agentic workflows. I'm comfortable moving between them depending on what the problem calls for.
